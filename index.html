Grammar Tips

Quick and simple grammar rules to help you speak and write correctly.

📌 To Be (am / is / are) Basic

Use am with I, is with he/she/it, and are with you/we/they.

✓ I am a student.
✓ She is happy.
✓ They are friends.
✗ I is a student.

📌 Articles (a / an / the) Basic

Use a before consonant sounds and an before vowel sounds. Use the for specific things.

✓ I have a dog.
✓ She eats an apple.
✓ The sun is bright.
✗ I have an dog.

📌 Present Simple Intermediate

Add -s or -es for he/she/it in present simple tense.

✓ He plays football.
✓ She watches TV.
✓ I like ice cream.
✗ She play football.

📌 Plural Nouns Basic

Most nouns add -s. Some add -es, -ies, or change form entirely.

✓ cat → cats
✓ box → boxes
✓ baby → babies
✓ child → children
